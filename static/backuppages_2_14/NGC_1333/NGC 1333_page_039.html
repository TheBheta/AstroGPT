NGC 1333

By
frazbot
Published: Feb 27, 2012
2.1K Views

Description

Located in the constellation Perseus, this is a close crop of a star forming region in the reflection nebula, NGC 1333. Particularly interesting features in this area are the pink glowing regions of gas, caused by jets of radiation from newly formed stars.

This is an 18" x 36" acrylic painting, see more details on my website!
