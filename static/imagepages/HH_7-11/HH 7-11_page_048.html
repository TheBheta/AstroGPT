Thomas and Friends (Season 7 - 11) Review

By
Stephen-Fisher
Published: May 22, 2021
3.4K Views

Description

Thomas and his friends had went a bit of a derailment, but not too much. Therefore, these seasons are not as charmful as the first six seasons.

Thomas goes on into some silly, and some extremely funny, but some bad stuff. The series' quality has gotten very fluctuant after the classic era. Let's start off with the seventh season.

Season 7, while it is very good, however they completely changed the music from Mike and Junior in place of Robert Hartshorne and the theme song was also changed from the Classic intro to the Season 8 intro. Seriously, what was the point of changing the seventh season into some new version instead of using the Season 1-6 style version? What's good about the series that we got Emily, which she is amazing engine in the series. She even her own song too.

Season 8, Season 8 is pretty good and has a handful of good episodes and is the strongest season of HiT Model Era, but it does have some inconsistencies and plot holes, like the infamous plot hole regarding Henry being bad at pulling the express. That sounds pretty silly and cause confusion and delay, right? Other problems could be the fact that numerous side characters from previous seasons such as Duck, BoCo, Oliver, Bill, Ben, Donald and Douglas are either nowhere to be seen or only make small cameo appearances.

Seasons 9 & 10, is the first two average series of the series. There are not a good as the previous 8 seasons. Such as:
  • Edward Strikes Out" (the worst offender of the model era)
  • "Emily Knows Best"
  • "Skarloey the Brave"
  • "Follow that Flour"
  • "Wharf and Peace"
  • "Toby Feels Left Out"
  • "The Magic Lamp"
  • And some more.
Season 11, is a good improvement over Seasons 9-10, even though it isn't as good as the previous 8 seasons, It can get a little predictable and repetitive at times, and has some bad episodes, not to mention the very yellowish lighting is somewhat off looking. However, this season has nowhere near as bad as Season 12.

Overall, these seasons are not the best, but we got a very infamous seasons 12-16, which were extremely hurtful to the series.
