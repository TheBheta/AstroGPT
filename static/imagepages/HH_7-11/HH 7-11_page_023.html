H H 711 Pill - beige capsule/oblong, 12mm

Pill with imprint H H 711 is Beige, Capsule/Oblong and has been identified as Paroxetine Hydrochloride 20 mg. It is supplied by Solco Healthcare U.S., LLC.

Paroxetine is used in the treatment of Anxiety and Stress; Anxiety; Depression; Generalized Anxiety Disorder; Hot Flashes and belongs to the drug class selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors. excluding Brisdelle: There is positive evidence of human fetal risk during pregnancy. Brisdelle: Not for use in pregnancy. Paroxetine 20 mg is not a controlled substance under the Controlled Substances Act (CSA).
